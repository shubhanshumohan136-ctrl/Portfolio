About

A guided design process, built around your lifestyle and goals.

Hi, I’m Shubhanshu Mohan

I’m an interior designer creating thoughtful, functional, and visually cohesive interiors. I use a clear design process—from understanding your lifestyle and goals to developing the concept, refining the details, and supporting the final execution—so your space feels “right” in everyday life, not just on paper.

I’m skilled in AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Lumion, which helps me visualize ideas clearly and communicate design direction effectively. I also hold a Master Diploma Architecture (CAD) certificate, strengthening my technical foundation for interior planning and presentation.

I’m especially interested in projects where layout, lighting, and material choices come together to create a space that looks great and works effortlessly day to day.

How I work

  1. Discovery Understand your goals, needs, and constraints.
  2. Concept Develop style direction, layout, and atmosphere.
  3. Design development Refine details and align materials + lighting.
  4. Final direction Support the final implementation-ready guidance.
